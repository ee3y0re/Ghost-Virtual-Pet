Sassper the Sassy Ghost

    • Your task is to attend to the needs and whim of the undead... or else...

    • Just kidding~ (maybe?)

    • When the ghost gets hangry, feed the ghost deadfruit.

    • To clean the ghost, wishy washy away the poop! (Yes, ghosts poop stars. Don't you believe in Santa Claus?)

    • Level up your basic bonding with the ghost to be able to finally show affection~

    • Just remember, ghosts are very territorial so be very weary of trying to hug a phantom that's never met you before.
